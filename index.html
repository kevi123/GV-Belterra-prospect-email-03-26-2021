What is The Granville Relief Project?

For March, the Granville Relief Project is matching dollar-for-dollar up to $15,000 in contributions made to Live Again Fresno. These critical funds will help Live Again Fresno with their mission to improve the conditions for children living on Parkway Drive, an area of Fresno known for drugs and prostitution.

As of March 26, we have raised $12,725 towards our goal of $15,000. If you would like to make a donation to help us reach our goal, please visit the Granville Relief Project.
